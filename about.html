Where clarity meets innovation

AquaCare was born to reinvent pool maintenance — merging AI-driven chemical balancing with a mobile-first ecosystem. We believe every pool deserves effortless brilliance.

OUR JOURNEY

The wave of transformation

Founded by aquatic engineers and tech visionaries, AquaCare emerged from a simple idea: pool owners shouldn't struggle with murky water or toxic chemicals. Today, we serve thousands of residential and commercial pools with precision sensors, eco-friendly chemistry, and real‑time mobile insights.

Our fleet of smart service units combine robotic cleaning and automated chemical injection, reducing waste and delivering the cleanest, safest swimming experience. Every drop of innovation carries our promise: smarter chemistry, sustainable future.
